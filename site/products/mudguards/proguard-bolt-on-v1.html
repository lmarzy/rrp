The ProGuard – BOLT ON is the first large direct mount mudguard of its type, it offers outstanding protection with the slickest most factory look available. There are no cable ties or hook and loop straps to ruin the clean lines on your bike as they are secured by bolts (supplied). We also have TILT BRACKETS which allow you to fine tune the fit angle of the mudguard, you can tilt the front/back up or down a few millimetres with 2 brackets (supplied).

DESIGN FEATURES IN MORE DETAIL

RUBBER PADS

The sides of the mudguard are flared out to touch and hold onto the fork to dampen resonance and create more stability, thick rubber pads have been included for the sides of the mudguard to protect the fork

BOLTS

We have designed our own custom bolts for ease of use and to ensure we have the most secure fit. The following bolts come supplied in the packaging:

  • M3 x 2 (FOX / MARZOCCHI)
  • M4 x 3 (OHLINS)
  • M6 x 1 (SR SUNTOUR)

TILT BRACKETS

We have included two TILT BRACKETS that just clip onto the front of the bracket allowing you to raise or lower the front for a better angle if needed.

ALLEN KEYS

2 Allen keys have been provided in the v1 packaging and only 1 in the v2 packaging as all the bolts were re-designed around a 3mm hex allen key

REAR FLICK

The raised rear flick was designed to simply offer a little bit more protection without adding extra length, it prevents more mud and spray from flying up the side of the frame especially when the wheel is turned

STICKER INDENT

The centre indent is a nice easy reference to apply our coloured stickers to, this indent also prevents the sticker from sitting proud on the surface of the guard which would leave it vulnerable to durability issues. The adhesive used is very special and super strong and can be jet washed

SEAL SHIELD

Our widely acclaimed Seal Shield offers greater protection for your fork seals from high velocity mud and spray lengthening service intervals

MATERIAL

We make our mudguard material thicker than the competition out there which together with our unique polypropylene mix allows massive impact protection, flexibility, and importantly cold weather durability and strength.

NB: If you don’t like the gloss highlights then spray the mudguard with GT85 and rub, this will remove the high gloss finish

Additional bolt packs can be purchased should you need them

MODIFYING THE ORIGINAL BOLT ON (V1)

OHLINS RXF38 WITH XL CSU (measuring 70mm)

Some Ohlins RXF38 forks have an extra-large top crown (CSU) measuring approximately 66-70mm (usually found on ebikes), these 2 sizes don’t clear the mudguard on the original Bolt On v1 if it’s not modified when using the RXF38 Adapter Bracket, the standard size crown 60mm should clear fine.

You can see if your fork is the standard or extra-large version of CSU by simply measuring the widest point of the round top crown see below:

  1. Approximately 60mm = STANDARD
  2. Approximately 66mm & 70mm = EXTRA-LARGE (XL)

NB: after modification and fitting test your bottom out clearance by removing the air from your fork and slowly depressing the fork to see if it clears the bracket to ensure its safe to use.

If yours is the XL version then you can modify your bracket to create more bottom out clearance when using some of our Adapter Brackets, you just need to cut off the centre supports as shown below with a sharp blade which will not affect your warranty.

IMPORTANT: Adults only for this modification, be careful not to slip and puncture the mudguard or hurt yourself

Fitting the ProGuard BOLT ON

A FULL LIST OF COMPATIBLE FORKS CAN BE FOUND HERE

FITTING TO THE FRONT:

  1. IMPORTANT: You must apply the rubber adhesive side pads to the recess on the mudguard side wings, they protect your fork legs and provide vital stability and resonance damping

  2. Clean the fork threads and the fork legs MAKING SURE YOU DRY THE THREADS THOROUGHLY

  3. Select the correct bolts for your fork (details below) and run these into the threads WITHOUT the mudguard which will 'PRIME' and clean the threads before fitting, this part is important(YOU MAY NEED TO RE-TAP THE THREADS IF DEBRIS / PAINT IS LEFT IN THE THREADS FROM THE MANUFACTURE OF THE FORKS)

    MOST COMMON BOLTS: FOX & MARZOCCHI = M3 | OHLINS = M4 | EXT ERA & SOME XFUSION = M5 | SR SUNTOUR = M6

    Some SR SUNTOUR forks have an unthreaded straight through hole in the centre of the fork brace, you can purchase a SLEEVE PACK FITTING KIT to allow the PROGUARD BOLT ON to fit these forks (sold separately)

  4. Squeeze the side wings of the mudguard hard and slide under the fork brace from the rear with the wheel and tyre still in place
  5. You should be able to locate the mudguard and let go as the rubber side pads should hold it in place (ensure the slots are centrally aligned to the threaded holes on your fork)
  6. Use the Allen key provided to screw the bolts in EVENLY a bit each side, go gentle to start off to ensure you DON'T CROSS THREAD THEM, squeeze the side wings periodically during tightening to break the seal so it can move

  7. Check alignment of the mudguard to ensure the distance from the tyre is even front and back, if you need to adjust the alignment then remove the mudguard and clip-on the appropriate TILT BRACKET found in the packaging (see example below). There are 2 TILT BRACKETS, one raises the front of the mudguard up and the other lowers it down (written on the TILT BRACKET)

  8. You can also change the height of the mudguard through the slots for more clearance. NOTE: Highest position offers the most stability with the best mud clearance, check bottom out clearance before use.

  9. Finally tighten the bolts to the torque settings below:

    TORQUE SETTINGS:
    M3 / M4 / M5 = 0.5nm to 1nm MAXIMUM | M6 = 2nm

NOTE: ONLY USE THE RRP SUPPLIED BOLTS AS THESE ARE SPECIFICALLY SELECTED OR DESIGNED FOR THE MUDGUARD. REPLACEMENT BOLTS PACKS ARE AVAILIBLE.

IMPORTANT - YOU MUST CHECK BOTTOM OUT CLEARANCE, LET THE AIR OUT OF YOUR FORK AND COMPRESS TO TEST IF THE FORK HITS THE MUDGUARD WHEN BOTTOMING. LOWER THE MUDGUARD THROUGH THE SLOTS IF YOU NEED MORE CLEARANCE, IF YOU CAN'T LOWER FURTHER THEN DO NOT USE

Important

  • Bolts must be checked before each ride to ensure the mudguard is secure
  • Remove mudguard every few rides to clean the brace / threads / fork legs / rubber pads
  • DO NOT USE IN STICKY / CLAGGY MUD AS IT MAY BLOCK AND CAUSE INJURY OR DAMAGE
  • MIN CLEARANCES - 15mm from the tyre when fitted and 10mm from the frame (on bottoming)
  • INSPECT BEFORE YOU RIDE: DO NOT USE IF DAMAGED IN ANY WAY AS CONTINUED USE MAY LEAD TO FAILURE OR INJURY
